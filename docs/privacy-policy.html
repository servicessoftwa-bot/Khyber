Privacy Policy  |  Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Khyber App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Khyber mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information – name, email address, and phone number when you register.
  • Location data – your approximate or precise location to show nearby services, transport routes, hospitals, and businesses. Location is only accessed when the app is in use.
  • Device information – device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostics and crash reporting.
  • Usage data – pages viewed and features used, to improve the app experience.
  • Photos / camera – only when you choose to upload a profile picture or attach an image to a listing. We do not access your camera without your permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise app features (food, transport, hospitals, jobs, etc.).
  • To process orders and bookings made through the app.
  • To send important service notifications (order updates, emergency alerts).
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations in Pakistan.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers – third-party vendors (e.g. map providers, SMS gateways) who help us operate the app, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements – if required by Pakistani law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business listings – when you contact a business through the app, your contact request is forwarded to that business only.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers (MongoDB Atlas). We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as required by law.

5. Location Permissions

The app requests location permission to show relevant local services. You can deny or revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. Core features will still work without location access.

6. Children's Privacy

The Khyber app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • OpenStreetMap / flutter_map – for map display
  • MongoDB Atlas – for data storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (if enabled) – for push notifications

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information from within the app.
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications.
  • Request deletion of all your data by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
